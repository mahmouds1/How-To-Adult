Welcome!

Welcome to How To Adult. Here you can find various tutorial videos to help with tasks you might not have learned from family or from school. If you have suggestions for videos we should include on our website feel free to fill out a form to submit a video for us to review. If you have a suggested topic we should focus on please fill out the associated form and we will review your suggestion! In the future we plan to include blog posts, websites and more to help get information our there.



About Us

We are two college students who will be graduating without knowing some common skills that we will need when we go out into the world. We chose to develop this website for those who were under similar circumstances as us and to make discovering necessary tutorials easier for our users. Our goal is to simplify the process of googling videos and being redirected to YouTube and being overwhelmed with all the different videos to choose from. Here we plan to have one to two videos associated with a tutorial topic that provide the best information to learn the topic.
